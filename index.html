Stori Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 9, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Stori, an iOS application ("App") that allows users to generate AI-powered images from text prompts to create stories, comics, manga, anime, and other creative content. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, and share your information when you use our App.

We take your privacy seriously and are committed to transparency about the data we collect and how it is used. By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • User authentication data: Email address and user identifiers when you sign in with Apple
  • User-generated content: Images you generate, for user retrieval
  • App usage data: How you interact with our App, including features used and time spent

Non-Personal Information

  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Technical data: App performance metrics, crash reports, and debugging information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Core App Functionality

  • To create and deliver AI-generated images based on your text prompts
  • To maintain your user account and app preferences
  • To store your generated images for your access and retrieval
  • To process in-app purchases and manage your subscription status

Service Improvement

  • To analyze app usage patterns and improve our services
  • To fix bugs and technical issues
  • To develop new features based on user behavior

4. Third-Party Services We Use

Firebase

We use Firebase services provided by Google for essential backend functionality, including:

  • User authentication and account management
  • Cloud infrastructure to process image generation requests
  • Secure storage of user-generated content
  • Database services to maintain app functionality

Superwall

We use Superwall for:

  • Payment processing: To handle in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Paywall management: To present and manage subscription offerings
  • Analytics: To understand conversion rates and optimize our subscription offerings

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. You can request deletion of your data by selecting the "Delete Account" button in the Stori app, or contacting us at storycreationaiapp@gmail.com.

Images you generate are stored:

  • In the app for your access until you delete them
  • Firebase Storage for user retrieval
  • On your device if you choose to save them locally

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at storycreationaiapp@gmail.com

By using Stori, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
